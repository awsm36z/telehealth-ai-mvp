## üì± HOW TO GET DOCUMENTS SIGNED

### Method 1: DocuSign (RECOMMENDED)
**Time:** 15 minutes to set up, 5 min for each party to sign  
**Cost:** Free trial (3 envelopes) / $25/mo after  
**Steps:**
1. Go to docusign.com/free-trial
2. Upload agreement PDFs
3. Add signers (you + developer)
4. Set signing order (you first)
5. Send for signature
6. Both parties sign electronically
7. DocuSign sends certified PDFs

**Why best:** Professional, audit trail, timestamps, legally iron-clad ‚úÖ

### Method 2: Google Forms + PDF (FASTEST)
**Time:** 10 minutes total  
**Cost:** Free  
**Steps:**
1. Create Google Form with agreement checklist
2. Email form link + PDF agreements
3. Developer completes form (confirms terms)
4. Form responses = legal record
5. Both keep PDF copies

**Why simple:** Super fast, free, still legally binding ‚úÖ

- **HelloSign:** Professional, user-friendly, free tier
